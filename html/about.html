About me

Welcome to the portfolio of Marjoree Elvira Fargas, a web developer and motion graphics designer

I am a graduate of the University of California, Santa Barbara, on a journey to excel as a web-developer. As a naturally creative person, I was having trouble finding an area of focus that would utilize both my creativity and technical skills until the wonderful world of coding found me.

What skills can I offer

My skills include creating graphics, cartoons/animations, and problem-solving thinking. I am a natural problem-solver with an endless supply of creativity and curiosity. I started my minor in Art and Technology while pursuing a Mathematical Sciences degree, and that is when I first learned HTML, CSS and JavaScript. With a primary focus on creative applications of the fundamentals, our projects demanded constant "out-of-the-box" thinking. I then switched into the Film and Media Studies program from Mathematics after reaching 3-Dimensional Calculus. I wanted to apply my interests and learnings into a creative outlet, and discovered the very versatile language, JavaScript.

Fun Fact: I am also an Optician! While pursuing my degree, I worked as an on-campus optician to support and have done work in the greater Los Angeles area to support myself. I am the type of person willing to go above and beyond for my clients and my team.
